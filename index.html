Client Experience

Let's meet Guoqiang, one of the very first ShareCommunity users.

Guoqiang lived in one of the rural counties near Shanghai for decades with his family. He and his family grew and sold crops and vegetables for living, and he worked as a part-time contracted constructor.

Half a year ago, a government proposal titled "Notice of House Removal" changed the life of Guoqiang and his family. His house was demolished due to the rapid urbanization. Guoqiang and his family were relocated to a newly-built neighbourhood in the inner suburb of Shanghai.

As one of the rural-to-urban migrants, Guoqiang was nervous rather than excited, about the new life.

"I had no idea what my life would be like or about how to settle down", said Guoqiang, "my wife and I needed jobs. My mom was complaining about leaving the county and about having nothing to do at home. What worried me the most was finding a new school for [my daughter]. At the same time, there were all sorts of papers I had to fill out."

Knowing Guoqiang's situation, the neighbourhood committee introduced ShareCommunity as well as one of the on-site ambassadors, who helped Guoqiang navigate the website.

"I am so glad there is a website like ShareCommunity that offers so much information to people like me. And unlike those officers at Certificate Office, the ambassador was so nice and patient. I introduced ShareCommunnity to my pals on the construction site, and they all found it pretty useful. "
